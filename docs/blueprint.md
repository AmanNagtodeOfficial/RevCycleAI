# **App Name**: RevCycle AI

## Core Features:

- Smart Patient Registration: Use OCR to autofill patient data from insurance cards and IDs, autofetch from EHR, and detect duplicate patients using ML similarity matching.
- Real-Time Eligibility & Benefits Engine: Provide live insurance verification and AI summarization of deductible remaining, copay, and covered services.
- NLP-Based Clinical Understanding: Ingest doctor's notes, operative reports, and discharge summaries; extract diagnoses, procedures, modifiers, and medical necessity signals using NLP.
- AI Coding Copilot: Suggest ICD-10, CPT, HCPCS, and modifiers with confidence scores and explanations. This tool allows coders to accept, modify, or reject suggestions.
- Smart Claim Builder: Automatically create claims from structured and unstructured data, applying payer-specific rules dynamically.
- AI Claim Scrubber: Check for missing data, invalid combinations, payer policy mismatches, historical denial patterns, and medical necessity gaps. Provide a Denial Risk Score and AI-generated Fix Suggestions. Goal: clean claims on first submission.
- Intelligent Claim Tracker: Track claim status with a timeline (submitted, accepted, pending, paid, denied) and predict expected payment date using AI. Provide a Kanban-style claim board with filters.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, but not-too-saturated blue (#4681C7) evoking trust and intelligence. 
- Background color: A light, desaturated blue (#E8F0FE) providing a clean and professional feel.
- Accent color: A contrasting yellow-gold (#E5B849) to highlight key actions and information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif with a modern, neutral look.
- Use consistent, clean icons to represent claim statuses, risk levels, and action items.
- Employ a visual hierarchy with color-coded risk indicators (green/yellow/red) and clear data presentation.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to guide users through workflows and provide feedback on actions.
